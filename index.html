Who We Are

Shaping Engineers,
Inspiring Leaders

Founded in 1998, VK Institute of Technology is one of Tamil Nadu's most respected engineering institutions — built on the conviction that rigorous academics and human values together produce exceptional professionals.

Spread across a lush 120-acre campus in Chennai, VKIT houses state-of-the-art laboratories, a 3-lakh-volume library, innovation incubators, and world-class sports infrastructure. Our alumni lead engineering teams at Google, Microsoft, Zoho, and over 500 other organisations worldwide.

🎯

Our Vision

To be a globally recognised institution that transforms young minds into ethical, innovative, and socially responsible engineers.

🚀

Our Mission

Deliver excellence in education through outcome-based learning, industry partnerships, and a culture of research and entrepreneurship.

Admissions 2025–26

A transparent, merit-based process designed to be simple and stress-free.

How to Apply

1

Register Online

Fill out the application form with your personal, academic, and contact details.

2

Upload Documents

Submit mark sheets, transfer certificate, passport photo, and ID proof.

3

Counselling

Attend TNEA / Management Quota counselling based on your preferred programme.

4

Pay Fees & Enroll

Confirm your seat, pay the admission fee, and begin your journey at VKIT!

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✅ B.Tech: 10+2 with PCM / PCB, minimum 60%
  • ✅ MBA: Any UG degree with 50% marks + CAT/MAT
  • ✅ MCA: B.Sc / BCA with Mathematics, minimum 55%
  • ✅ NRI / Foreign students: Eligible via Management Quota
